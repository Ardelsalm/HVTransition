Strategic Rationale: Why the Pivot?

Our internal sales data clearly demonstrates that our most valuable and high-margin transactions consistently originate from premium furniture customization and bespoke interior design projects. The operational costs associated with our expansive showroom footprint, coupled with sales consistently falling below our OPEX, are no longer aligned with these core revenue drivers, contributing significantly to the ‚Çπ3 Crores loss incurred in the previous model.
