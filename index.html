Strategic Rationale: Why the Pivot?

Our internal sales data clearly demonstrates that our most valuable and high-margin transactions consistently originate from premium furniture customization and bespoke interior design projects. The operational costs associated with our expansive showroom footprint, coupled with sales consistently falling below our OPEX, are no longer aligned with these core revenue drivers, contributing significantly to the ₹3 Crores loss incurred in the previous model.

This evolution is a deliberate pivot towards a more focused, agile, and profitable business model, concentrating on where we truly excel and delivering superior value. The new studio hybrid format is specifically designed to recover past losses and drive substantial future growth.
